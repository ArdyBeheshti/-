About Me


My name is Ardeshir Alexander Beheshti. I am an GIS post-graduate from San Diego State University and currently a GIS programmer and database manager with AECOM, Inc. for the greater San Diego area.


The general focus of my works is the development and impact of renewable energy sources, the implementation of machine learning algorithms in behavioral health studies, and the development of autonmous technology.


During my undergraduate, I was given the opportunity to have worked with Dr. Ming-Hsiang Tsou in the Center of Human Dynamics in the Mobile Age (HDMA) at SDSU, and Dr. Bruce Appleyard at the Active Transportation Research at SDSU, where I focused on providing community-based research and planning services, developed GIS applications, and information diffusion in social/spatial networks.
