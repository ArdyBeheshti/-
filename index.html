About Me


Hello! My name is Ardeshir Alexander Beheshti. I am an Computer Science & Geographic Information Sciences post-graduate from San Diego State University and currently a GIS Developer with EDF-RE.


The general focus of my works is the development and impact of renewable energy sources, the implementation of machine learning algorithms in behavioral health studies, and the development of autonmous technology.


During my undergraduate, I was given the opportunity to have worked with Dr. Ming-Hsiang Tsou in the Center of Human Dynamics in the Mobile Age (HDMA) at SDSU, and Dr. Bruce Appleyard at the Active Transportation Research at SDSU, where I focused on providing community-based research and planning services, developed applications, and information diffusion in social/spatial networks.
